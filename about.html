Introduction

I am a French physics researcher. I moved to Montreal for my undergraduate studies. In December 2023, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science majoring in Physics and minoring in Computer Science from McGill University.

After graduation, I interned at the Kasler Brossel Laboratory in the Quantum Fluid of Light group under the supervision of Professor Quentin Glorieux in Paris, France. I developed a machine learning tool to better characterize quantum fluids in hot atomic vapor.

I am now back in Montreal for my graduate studies. I am a Ph.D candidate in Theoretical Physics in the research group of Professor Hong Guo (Lab) at McGill University.
